PLACEMENTS



Andhra University College of Engineering recorded (AUCoE) around 95% placement during Andhra University placements 2022.
Further, the placement rate recorded during Andhra University College of Engineering for Women (AUCEW) placements 2022 was 100%.
The leading company Cognizant recruited around 450 students in two phases during Andhra University placements 2022.
The highest package offered during Andhra University placements 2022 was between INR 13.5 LPA and INR 15 LPA.
Apart from this, more than 100 students from the Pharmacy and Chemistry departments were also placed during Andhra University placements 2022.
